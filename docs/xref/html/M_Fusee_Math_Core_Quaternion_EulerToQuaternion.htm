Remarks
The euler angle is assumed to be in common aviation order where the y axis is up. Thus x is pitch/attitude, y is yaw/heading, and z is roll/bank. In practice x is never out of [-PI/2, PI/2] while y and z may well be in the range of [-PI, PI]. See also the euclideanspace website.
